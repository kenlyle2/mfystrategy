## 🔴 Facebook-Specific: What's Failing

### Link posts are invisible
Facebook has a post type IG doesn't: Links. The one link post in 90 days got **0.02% engagement** — 67% below the already-low Facebook average. Facebook's algorithm demonstrably suppresses outbound links. Dean uses this format rarely, but the data confirms it should be zero.

### Video is less dominant on Facebook
On Instagram, video is 80% of posts but only **0.69× efficient** (severely underperforms). On Facebook, video is 78% of posts and **0.96× efficient** — nearly on par with single images. Facebook's algorithm is friendlier to video reach.

| Format | IG Efficiency | FB Efficiency |
|---|---|---|
| Album / Carousel | 2.19× | **2.02×** |
| Single Image | **3.10×** | 1.00× |
| Video | 0.69× | 0.96× |
| Link | n/a | 0.24× |

**Key difference:** On Instagram, single images dramatically outperform video. On Facebook, the gap nearly disappears. Albums/Carousels win on *both* platforms by a wide margin.

### Facebook's audience is strongly weekend-centric; Instagram's is not

- **Facebook:** Weekend avg **0.106%** vs weekday avg 0.057% → **weekend-centric** (+86% better)
- **Instagram:** Weekend avg 0.194% vs weekday avg 0.218% → **balanced** (weekdays slightly better)

The "Man Flow Yoga crowd is weekend-centric" hypothesis is **true for Facebook, false for Instagram**. They are two different audience cohorts with different weekly behaviors — even though they follow the same account.

## 🎯 ZeroDollarBlueprint Strategic Summary

**Channel strategy:** Build the relationship on Instagram (3.5× more engaged per follower). Use Facebook for reach to a larger, more passive weekend audience. Don't treat them as the same channel.

**Content hierarchy (works on both, in order):** Personal vulnerability → Taboo health topic with specific frame → Real-time event or moment → Actionable specific routine → Everything else.

**Format:** Lead with carousels/albums on both platforms. They're the highest-efficiency format across the board. Videos are acceptable on Facebook; they're a drag on Instagram.

**Posting schedule:**
- IG: Wednesday + Friday (avoid Tue/Sun absolutely)
- FB: Saturday + Wednesday + Monday (avoid Fri/Tue/Sun)

**Hashtag playbook:** Use outcome-specific, slightly edgy tags (#yogaforstiffness, #yogafored, #yogaforsex, #redlighttherapy). Avoid the obvious branded and category tags everyone uses (#manflowyoga, #yogaforbackpain).

**The ZeroDollarBlueprint angle maps perfectly to what wins:** The "I tried the expensive longevity approach and found a cheaper path" is inherently personal, slightly taboo (mortality anxiety), and specific. That's the content DNA of every high-performing post in this dataset.
